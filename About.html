Marc Douglas was born and raised in New Jersey. He graduated from Vermont College and shortly after started and ran his own automated solutions sales company based out of New Jersey, New York and then North Carolina for over 20 years. Marc Douglas always had a passion for writing and began to pursue and refine his craft upon retirement and sale of his company.

Marc Douglas lives in North Carolina with his wife of 40+ years, Susan, and their labrador retriever mix, Louise. Marc and his wife have 2 children who are his biggest fans, and a first grandchild coming early next year.
